Cosine Similarity between Painters

  • TFiDF adjusted term frequency distribution is calculated for each artist.

  • Latent Semantic Indexing (SVD) is used to linearly project the term frequency distribution to a lower dimensional (50) space.

  • Cosine similarities between artists are calculated.
